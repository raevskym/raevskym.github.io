66% of U.S. electricity is wasted, contributing to a $130 billion energy waste per year.

Mitigating this waste is prohibitively expensive for most consumers – switching to high efficiency A/Cs costs or installing solar panels costs upwards $2000.



root is a $35 outlet attachment that saves time, money, and the environment.


By turning your smartphone into a remote control, root constructs Wi-Fi geo-fences that enable automated location-based control.


Using its built-in thermostat, root can also be used with plug-in ACs and space heaters to automate temperature. root learns your preferred temperature schedule in a day and continues to improve over time, resulting in a comfortable schedule when home, and a cost-saving one when away.

root can pay for itself in as little as 2 months and accumulate over $100 savings per year
