“Not all those who wander are lost.” – J.R.R. Tolkien



This work aims to encourage and enable travel because doing so propels societal perspective, knowledge, happiness, and compassion.


But dude, why are you wasting your time developing this site when you could just upload to Facebook/Twitter/Insta?

Photographic storytelling is an art. I believe a blank canvas is a nessecary starting point to remove social media's psychological overshadowings and imerse the viewer in my journey. I aim to share the continuous legacy my trips leave on my psyche, rather than discrete moments in time.
